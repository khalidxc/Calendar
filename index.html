KFUPM Calendar


Description:

KFUPM Calendar is a normal calendar with basic functionalities, but its main objective is unique; mainly will be used for resolving major/midterm conflicts.

The main users are:

  • Instructors
  • Students

The idea is: every Instructor will create a general event (major/midterm/quiz) and this event will appear for every student he teaches in his section, so if every instructor created his "events", the students will have a clear picture of the semester.
